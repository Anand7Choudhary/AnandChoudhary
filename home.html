Portfolio

Movieium

Created a personal movie library website that's basically like having a really smart movie buddy who knows exactly what you like! This website uses some fancy machine learning (KNN) to analyze your favorite films and then recommends other movies that are similar, so you don't have to spend hours scrolling through Netflix. It's like having a personal film critic, except it won't judge you for re-watching your favorite rom-com for the tenth time! Plus, the website lets you save all your fave films and even serves up more tailored recommendations the more you use it. It's basically the perfect movie date, minus the awkward silences and uncomfortable popcorn-sharing.

FoodoPedia

I once created a Windows application that was basically a foodie's dream come true. With this app, users could register and search for their desired dishes, rate multiple meals, create a personalized recipe library, and even write their own food blog! The best part? The users had complete control to be their own Gordon Ramsay and create their very own unique and delicious recipes. So, it's basically a one-stop-shop for all your food needs, and it's so easy to use that even a chef who can't boil water (like me) can navigate it with ease!

PreDzn

Website where you can create the look of your website without any coding skills! It's like using Photoshop but for websites, except you're not limited to just images. You can add dynamic elements like text, divs, symbols and more, and edit them with ease. With this website, you'll never have to worry about being stuck with a boring pre-design again, and you can finally let your creativity run wild! It's the perfect tool to save time and avoid the hassle of drawing your website design on paper. So why settle for a bland and boring website, when you can have one that's uniquely you!
